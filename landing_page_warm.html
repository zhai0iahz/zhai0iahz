Meet Jason

Jason is a educator, philosopher, and performer who genuinely believes tennis should be for everyone. He specializes in helping beginners—especially those from non-athletic backgrounds—discover the joy and confidence that comes with learning tennis the right way.

Located near Irvine Spectrum Center, Jason works with academics, gamers, artists, and unconventional thinkers who might never have imagined themselves on a tennis court. He meets you exactly where you are and helps you grow from there.

"I am an advocate for finding one's own tennis, no matter how unconventional."

His philosophy is simple but powerful: Fuel the passion, Burn the waste, Save the lessons. Every lesson with Jason is about building confidence, discovering your authentic style, and creating lasting joy in the game.

The Court & Craft Way

Here's what makes learning with Jason different and so welcoming:

🎮 Gaming Mechanics

We explain tennis through frameworks you already understand—levels, progression, strategy. Makes it click faster and feels more natural.

🎨 Your Style

There's no "right way" to be a tennis player. We help you find your authentic style and build it with confidence.

💭 Psychology First

Self-doubt and anxiety are normal. Jason helps you understand and overcome them with proven mental techniques.

🤗 Beginner-Friendly

No athletic background? Perfect. We specialize in bringing newcomers into tennis with joy, warmth, and zero judgment.

📚 Mentorship

Jason isn't just a coach—he's a mentor invested in your growth as a person, not just a player.

🔥 Fuel. Burn. Save.

Fuel your passion for tennis. Burn away bad habits and self-doubt. Save the lessons for a lifetime of growth.
